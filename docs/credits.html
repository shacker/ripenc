RipEnc: Credits and Thanks

The following individuals have contributed suggestions, code snippets, and in some cases, complete functions somewhere in the evolution of RipEnc. Thanks!
  • Marco Nelissen (marcon@xs4all.nl) for the amazing SoundPlay, cdda-fs, and cddblinkd (the latter two are now owned by Be, Inc., though cddblinkd is open source)
  • Helge Philipp (helge.philipp@fortytwo.uni-oldenburg.de): VBR support.
  • Jason Simpson (jason@xio.com): Frequency output options.
  • Jamie Wilkinson (jamie@tave.com): Hand-naming routines and suggestions (no longer used).
  • Graham Gilmore (grahamg@videotron.ca): Bug fixes in CD-ROM detection.
  • Jon Watte (hplus@mindcontrol.org): Bug fixes and suggestions in space handling, directory moving, and handling of enhanced CDs.
  • Luke A. Kanies (lkanies@reed.edu) suggested option to disable filename extensions.
  • Ari Pernick (pernick@meat.net): id3attr (no longer used by RipEnc).
If you've contributed and I've forgotten to add your name, please remind me!
